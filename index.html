WHO WE ARE

PlusPlus Capital Management

PlusPlus Capital Management is a quantitative trading firm that trades futures and options on futures all across the globe. The firm models the behavioral inefficiencies of market participants using its extensive database of historical futures prices and options data, and constructs strategies to exploit such inefficiencies. The firm's trading decisions are driven exclusively by data, and trading is completely automated. The managing partners bring over a decade of experience in senior-level positions at large futures trading firms.

In 2014, PlusPlus Capital Management was profiled in CTA Intelligence magazine as one of the hottest prospects of the futures trading industry, and has also been profiled in HFM Week magazine.  The managing partners also recently completed a podcast with the CME Group on short-term futures trading.**
